Windows Media Center

Windows Media Center is a multi media application that runs on your PC. I was involved in several releases of the application including the final Windows 7 release. I Led UX feature teams in creation of scenarios, storyboards, and final prototypes and worked with cross functional feature teams to develop much of the application. I directed animation designers, design developers, and sound designers during production and development of the app.
