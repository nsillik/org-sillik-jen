What I Do

I get down and dirty with wireframes, lead teams of talented people towards hard deadlines,

and make the tough decisions, all while advocating the best experience for the user.
User Experience Design

As a UX designer I'm passionate about making products that are simple and intuitive for the end user. I’ve led many different types of projects through all of their phases. I take pride in my job, facilitating the creative minds around me and making their ideas come to life.

User Interaction Design

Communication of ideas is one of the most important roles I play as a UI designer. I use a variety of tools including site maps, flow charts, diagrams, wireframes, and detailed documentation to communicate my ideas effectively.

Visual Design

I’ve designed and art-directed many projects, creating a compelling visual experience for end users. In addition to visual design I’ve done a range of production specs and asset creation.

About Me

I am a user experience designer & a passionate user, so I know how important it is to get it right.

I have led and been involved in many blue sky strategy projects, but my real love is shipping products. I value being part of a product team: coordinating with business teams to create the right products, assisting researchers to test ideas, and working closely with engineers to come up with real solutions.

Teamwork is one of my greatest passions. I value the experience and knowledge of my peers. Working together as a product team is important to generate trust and respect, which leads to a good working environment and great products.
